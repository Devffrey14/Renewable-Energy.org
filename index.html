about us

What is our goal?

This website was created by a group of students from the University of Energy and Natural Resources (UENR) in Sunyani-Ghana, with the sole purpose of aiding people in the search of renewable energy sources. Thorough reasearch was conducted by the students in order to find Governmental, Non-Governmental Organizations and Private Instituions which support the Affordable and Renewable Energy initiative. The goal of this website is to connect people to such organizations through their social medial handles with relative ease and to also connect entrepreneurs who are looking to gain suport in the clean energy sector.

Some of the Organizations

Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Partnership (REEEP) Ghana

Works on promoting renewable energy and energy efficiency projects in Ghana

Solar Light Company Ltd

Provides solar solutions including solar panels, solar water heaters, and solar street lights.

Global Energy Solutions Ghana

Provides renewable energy solutions such as solar power systems and energy-efficient appliances.

Ghana Alliance for Clean Cookstoves

Focuses on promoting clean cooking solutions, including efficient and renewable energy cookstoves.

Ghana Climate Innovation Center (GCIC)

Supports entrepreneurs and businesses in the clean energy sector by providing funding, training, and mentorship.

Husk Power Systems Ghana

Implements mini-grids powered by renewable energy sources, such as solar and biomass, to provide electricity to rural communities.
